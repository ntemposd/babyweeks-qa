About Babyweeks

Why I construction built this app.

Throughout the last year we are experiencing parenthood. As our baby grows she reveals some killer mood changes, attributed to development transitions happening during the weeks between her development cycle stages, according to her pediatricians. This app helps my partner to calculate the week of our daughter's growing cycle in 3 clicks, rather than manually counting the weeks one by one in her mobile calendar.

Can anyone use this app?

Absolutely! The app is free, open-source and runs on your browser. Meaning it doesn't store any personal or sensitive data such as emails and your baby's birthdate so that you are targeted with ads later on. The app uses essential cookies to track information like your location, browser and button clicks exploited by the creator to optimize the app's behavior and functionality. Don't want to share those? Here is how to opt-out.
